Incident Reporting and Investigation Process

Employees can report psychological incidents via a confidential reporting system. HR and safety teams review reports, conduct investigations, and identify root causes. Corrective actions are implemented, and affected employees receive ongoing support. Documentation ensures transparency and future prevention.

Mechanisms to Review and Continuously Improve the PHSMS

Annual audits, employee feedback, and KPIs help monitor the PHSMS. Policies are updated based on benchmarking and lessons learned from incidents. Leadership reviews and third-party assessments ensure alignment with best practices.
